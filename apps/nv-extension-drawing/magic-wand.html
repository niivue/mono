Click on a slice to segment a region.
Usage: Select "Magic Wand" mode, then click on a 2D slice to flood-fill connected voxels with similar intensity. Threshold mode controls range: Symmetric = seed ± tolerance, Percent = seed × (1 ± pct), Bright/Dark = one-sided fill, Auto = auto-detect from cal min/max. Set Max dist to limit fill radius in mm. Scroll wheel changes slices.
