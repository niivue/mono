2D slices: left-drag to paint, or with Fill draw a closed loop to flood, or with Wand click to grow an intensity-similar region (2D confines it to the slice; off grows the whole 3D structure).  |  3D exploded blocks: right-drag to paint a tube; with Fill right-click floods the blob; with Wand right-click grows the region.  |  Pen -> Vector (SVG): draw freehand vector polygons on a 2D slice, or right-drag directly on a 3D block, then SVG to export them.  |  Clip toggles a cutaway from its own control, so right-drag keeps drawing on the visible blocks. Alt+right-drag rotates the clip plane without leaving the draw mode.
